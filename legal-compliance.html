Legal & Compliance Framework

POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act)

POPIA is a key piece of legislation that governs how we handle personal information. Compliance is mandatory, and failure to comply can result in significant penalties.

Maximum Penalties: R10 million fine and/or 12 months imprisonment.

8 Conditions for Lawful Processing:

  • Accountability: We are responsible for ensuring compliance.
  • Processing Limitation: We only process necessary information.
  • Purpose Specification: Data is collected for a specific, defined purpose.
  • Further Processing Limitation: Data is not used for a secondary purpose without consent.
  • Information Quality: We ensure data is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Openness: We are transparent about our data practices.
  • Security Safeguards: We protect data against loss, damage, or unauthorized access.
  • Data Subject Participation: Individuals have the right to access and correct their data.

Source: Official POPIA Act
